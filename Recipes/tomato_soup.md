# Tomato Soup

## Tags
- Soup
- Lunch

## Ingredients

* 1 can tomatoes
* 1 yellow onion
* 1/4 cup butter

## Instructions

1. Add butter, tomatoes, chopped onion, and water to a large pot.
2. Bring everything to a low simmer and cook for 40 minutes.
3. Blend the soup until smooth with an immersion blender.
4. Serve and enjoy!
